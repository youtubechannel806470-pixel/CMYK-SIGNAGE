Technology & Capability

Powered by Modern Production

Our workshop combines advanced printing, fabrication and finishing technologies to ensure high-quality execution across every branding project.

Plotter Cut Machine

High-precision cutting machine used for vinyl, flex, stickers, and various branding materials with clean and accurate finishing.

Panel Saw UPS 3200 A HV

Advanced panel cutting machine designed for smooth and accurate cutting of ACP, MDF, acrylic, and plywood sheets.

Hydraulic Cold Press – 2 Cylinders

Heavy-duty hydraulic press machine used for strong and uniform bonding of laminates and panel materials.

Automatic Edgebander UEB 3.1

Fully automatic edge banding machine that provides seamless and professional edge finishing for MDF and plywood panels.

High Speed Auto Edgebander

High-speed edge banding solution ensuring fast, clean, and durable finishing for large-scale production work.

Lamination Machine

Professional lamination machine used to protect and enhance prints, vinyl, flex, and branding graphics.

Solvent Print Machine

Large-format printing machine delivering vibrant, weather-resistant, and high-resolution prints for outdoor and indoor branding.

Eco Solvent Print Machine

Eco-friendly printing machine ideal for premium-quality stickers, vinyl graphics, posters, and signage applications.

UV Print & Cut Print Machine

Advanced UV printing and contour cutting machine for high-quality graphics, labels, stickers, and branding materials.

CNC Router Machine

Precision CNC machine used for cutting, carving, and engraving on ACP, acrylic, MDF, PVC, and wood materials.

Laser Cut Machine

High-accuracy laser cutting and engraving machine for detailed work on acrylic, wood, MDF, and decorative materials.

UV Flat Bed Machine

State-of-the-art UV flatbed printer capable of direct printing on rigid surfaces like acrylic, glass, metal, wood, and PVC.
